Terms of use

The Daily Abstract is offered for free, as-is, by an individual. Here are the basic rules of the road.

What you get

  • A daily email digest of new arXiv papers matching the categories and keywords you picked at subscription.
  • The ability to reply with paper numbers and receive the PDFs as attachments.
  • A web interface to manage and unsubscribe at any time.

What we don't promise

  • No uptime SLA. The service runs on a free-tier VM and can go down. We do our best.
  • No guarantee of delivery. Email goes through third-party SMTP providers (Brevo) and can be rate-limited, throttled, or marked as spam by your provider.
  • No guarantee of arXiv coverage. The arXiv API sometimes rate-limits requests; on those days the digest may be empty or delayed.
  • No warranty. We provide this service "as is".

Acceptable use

  • Use your own email. Don't subscribe other people.
  • Don't try to abuse the subscribe form (we'll block obvious abuse).
  • Don't scrape, automate or republish the digests at scale without asking.

Open source

The code is open source on GitHub. You can self-host your own instance and not depend on us at all.
