How it works

  1. You subscribe. Pick categories and (optionally) keywords. We send a confirmation email — single click to activate.
  2. Each morning we ship. One fetch to arXiv, filtered for your set, translated to Spanish, emailed at 7am.
  3. You reply with numbers. "1, 3, 5" — and the PDFs of those papers come back attached to the reply.
  4. Manage anytime. Every digest has a link to change your categories or unsubscribe.

FAQ

What does it cost?
Nothing. The whole stack runs on Oracle Cloud's always-free tier and we use free quotas of Brevo for outbound email and Mailsac for reply parsing. If it gets popular we may add a Pro tier for power features, but the basic digest stays free.
Who's behind this?
An indie, open-source project. Source on GitHub. No company. No ads. No data resold.
How do I get the PDFs?
Reply to the digest email with the numbers of the papers you want — "1 3 5", "the second one", or "quiero el 2 y 7". The listener parses the numbers, downloads from arXiv, and replies with the PDFs attached. Usually within 30 seconds.
How do I cancel?
Every email has a one-click unsubscribe link in the footer. You can also reply with "STOP" or write to the address shown there.
